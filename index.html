About

Hello, I'm Sarah!
I'm 25 years old and I’m finishing a training course in front-end web development at Interface3.
Before embarking on this adventure, I was a waitress and barmaid in an Ethiopian restaurant, Kokob. There, I learned to be organized, active and to work as a team. In parallel, I followed a training course in professional and artistic makeup at the « Institut des Arts et Métiers ». During this training, I was able to fully express my creativity.
In early 2018, I wanted to bring more depth and meaning to my professional life by starting a new formation in web development, where I can now combine creative, modern and technical work.
And here I am today, armed with my skills, in search of the internship that will allow me to blossom in this new way.
